About Me

Currently pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Business Marketing at Juniata College, I’ve developed a foundation in marketing principles, consumer behavior, and campaign planning. I am especially passionate about applying these skills in the financial services industry—particularly within investment firms—where I can help expand their market reach through consumer analytics and strategic market research. I am excited by the potential to use marketing as a tool to communicate complex financial services in a clear, accessible, and engaging way. My goal is to contribute to a team where innovation, insight, and integrity drive measurable results.
